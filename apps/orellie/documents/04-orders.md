# Orders

Everything you need to know about order changes, cancellations, pre-orders, promotions, and store credit.

---

## Order Changes & Cancellations

We process orders quickly, so if you need to make a change or cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible.

If your order has not yet been packed or dispatched, we’ll do our best to help.

Once an order has been shipped, we’re unable to make changes or cancellations.

---

## Pre-Orders

Some items may be available for pre-order.

If a product is listed as a pre-order, the estimated dispatch timeframe will be shown on the product page.

Please note:

- Payment is taken at checkout
- Dispatch dates are estimates
- Delays can sometimes happen due to supplier or production changes

If your order includes both in-stock and pre-order items, your order may be shipped together once all items are ready.

---

## Promotions & Discount Codes

Discount codes must be entered at checkout and cannot usually be added after an order has been placed.

Unless stated otherwise:

- Only one discount code can be used per order
- Discount codes cannot be combined with other offers
- Discount codes do not apply to sale items

We reserve the right to change or end promotions at any time.

---

## Gift Cards & Store Credit

Gift cards and store credit can be used at checkout unless otherwise stated.

Please note:

- They are non-refundable
- They cannot be exchanged for cash
- They are non-transferable
- They must be used before any expiry date, if one applies

Lost or stolen gift cards cannot be replaced.
