# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 2026*

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what personal information Orellie collects, how we use it, and how we protect it.

---

## Who We Are

Orellie is an online earring store based in New Zealand, operating through **orellie.nz**.

---

## Information We Collect

When you shop with us, contact us, or browse our website, we may collect:

- Your name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Billing and shipping address
- Order details
- Payment and transaction information
- Messages you send to us
- Website usage information such as browser, device, IP address, and pages visited

Payment details are processed securely by our payment providers. We do not store full card details on our systems.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Process and deliver your orders
- Send order updates and shipping confirmations
- Respond to customer enquiries
- Improve our website, products, and services
- Send marketing emails if you have subscribed
- Meet legal and accounting obligations

---

## Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve functionality, remember preferences, and understand how visitors use the site.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect how the site works.

---

## Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We may share your information with trusted third-party providers who help us operate our business, such as:

- Payment providers
- Courier and delivery services
- Website hosting and analytics providers
- Email and marketing platforms

These providers only receive the information needed to perform their service.

We may also share information if required by law.

---

## Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

While we work hard to keep your information secure, no online system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

---

## Your Rights

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you and ask us to correct it if needed.

To make a privacy request, please contact us at **hello@orellie.nz**.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.
