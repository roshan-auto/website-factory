# Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your Orellie earrings. If something isn’t right, we’re here to help.

---

## Change of Mind

For hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns or exchanges on earrings that have been opened, worn, or removed from their original packaging.

If your item is completely unopened, unworn, and in its original packaging, please contact us within **7 days of delivery**. In some cases, we may offer a store credit or exchange at our discretion.

Sale and clearance items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged unless they are faulty.

---

## Faulty or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged, faulty, or incorrect, please contact us as soon as possible.

To help us resolve the issue quickly, please include:

- Your order number
- A short description of the problem
- Clear photos of the item

If a product is confirmed to be faulty or incorrect, we may offer a repair, replacement, exchange, or refund, depending on the issue.

---

## Return Conditions

For any approved return, the item must:

- Be in its original condition
- Be returned in its original packaging
- Include proof of purchase

Items showing signs of wear, misuse, accidental damage, or improper care are not considered faulty.

---

## How to Start a Return

Email us at **hello@orellie.nz** with your order number and details of the issue.

If your return is approved, we’ll provide the next steps.

For change-of-mind returns that are accepted, return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer. For confirmed faulty or incorrect items, return shipping costs may be covered by us.

---

## Refunds

Once your return has been received and assessed, we’ll confirm the outcome by email.

If approved, refunds are processed back to your original payment method. Please allow a few business days for your bank or payment provider to complete the transaction.

Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the item was faulty or incorrect.
