# Jewellery Care

A little care goes a long way. Follow these simple tips to keep your Orellie earrings looking their best.

---

## Everyday Wear

- Put your earrings on last, after perfume, hairspray, skincare, and makeup
- Remove them before showering, swimming, exercising, or sleeping
- Avoid contact with water, sweat, lotions, and harsh chemicals

These simple habits help protect the finish and extend the life of your jewellery.

---

## Cleaning

For regular care, gently wipe your earrings with a soft, dry cloth.

If they need a deeper clean:

1. Use lukewarm water with a small amount of mild soap
2. Gently clean with a soft cloth or soft brush
3. Rinse carefully with clean water
4. Dry thoroughly before storing

Avoid abrasive materials, harsh cleaners, or rough polishing cloths.

---

## Storage

Store your earrings in a dry place away from direct sunlight.

We recommend keeping them in a jewellery box, pouch, or separate compartment to help prevent scratching, tangling, and tarnishing.

---

## Sensitive Ears

Material details are listed on each product page to help you choose what suits you best.

If you have sensitive ears, we recommend checking product materials before purchasing. If irritation occurs, stop wearing the earrings immediately.

---

## Plated Jewellery

Plated jewellery should be handled with extra care. Over time, plated finishes can naturally fade with wear.

To help preserve plating:

- Keep it dry
- Avoid chemicals and perfumes
- Store it carefully
- Clean gently and infrequently

---

## Damage

If your earrings become bent, loose, or damaged, stop wearing them and contact us for advice.

Normal wear and tear, including minor surface marks or natural fading of plated finishes over time, is not considered a manufacturing fault.
