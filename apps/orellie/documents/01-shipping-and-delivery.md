# Shipping & Delivery

We want your Orellie order to arrive quickly and safely. Here’s everything you need to know about how we ship.

---

## Processing Time

Orders are packed and dispatched within **1–2 working days** after payment is confirmed. We do not ship on weekends or public holidays, and during busy sale periods, processing may take a little longer.

If there’s ever an unexpected delay, we’ll let you know as soon as possible.

---

## Delivery Timeframes

### New Zealand

- **Urban addresses:** 1–3 working days
- **Rural addresses:** 3–7 working days

### International

- Delivery timeframes vary depending on the destination country and customs processing.

All delivery timeframes are estimates only and may occasionally be affected by courier delays, weather events, or customs processing outside of our control.

---

## Shipping Rates

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout before payment is completed.

From time to time, we may offer free shipping promotions. Any conditions will be clearly shown on our website.

Rural delivery charges may apply where required by the courier network.

---

## International Orders

We ship to selected international destinations. Availability and shipping rates are shown at checkout.

International orders may be subject to customs duties, taxes, or import fees charged by the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the customer.

If a parcel is refused or left unclaimed due to unpaid customs charges, the original shipping cost is non-refundable.

---

## Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you’ll receive a confirmation email with tracking details.

All parcels are sent with tracking. Some deliveries may require a signature on arrival.

---

## Incorrect Addresses

Please make sure your shipping address is correct before placing your order.

If a parcel is returned to us due to an incorrect or incomplete address, additional shipping charges may apply to resend it.
